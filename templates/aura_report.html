About AURA Engine

AURA Engine provides comprehensive environmental justice research and analysis, combining data science, community engagement, and urban planning expertise.

This report analyzes environmental conditions, health impacts, and design interventions for Alley 3 in the Pico-Union neighborhood.

Key Findings

The AURA Report provides evidence-based recommendations for transforming Alley 3 into a climate-resilient, community-centered public space.

Research demonstrates how integrated design interventions can address stormwater management, urban heat, air quality, and social cohesion simultaneously.

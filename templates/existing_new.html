Site Assessment Summary

Alley 3 presents a critical condition in its drainage infrastructure and a poor condition across its paving surface. Original concrete alley construction dates to approximately 1952. Standing water, root heave, and years of deferred maintenance have created safety hazards and stormwater compliance issues that the Innovation Alleys project aims to fully remediate.
